Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Nyumba Yatu

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by downloading the app and clicking on "Sign Up". You'll need to provide your email address, phone number, and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google account for faster registration.

How do I search for properties?

Use the search bar at the top of the app to enter your location, price range, and property type. You can also use filters to narrow down your search by amenities, number of bedrooms, and more.

How do I list my property?

To list your property, go to the "List Property" section in the app. Fill in all the required details including location, price, property type, and upload photos. Once submitted, your listing will be reviewed and published within 24 hours.

How do I contact a property owner?

You can contact property owners directly through the in-app messaging feature. Simply click on a property listing and select "Message Owner" to start a conversation.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the basic features of Nyumba Yatu are free to use. However, property owners may choose to use premium features for better visibility of their listings.

How do I save favorite properties?

You can save properties to your favorites by clicking the heart icon on any property listing. You can view all your saved properties in the "Favorites" section of your profile.

What should I do if I encounter a problem?

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team through the "Support" section in the app or email us at joshuakundamwape@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time by going to Settings > Account Settings > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
