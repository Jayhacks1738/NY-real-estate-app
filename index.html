About Nyumba Yatu

Whether you're a tenant looking for a house, a student searching for a boarding house, a business in need of an office space, or a property owner wanting to list your space, Nyumba Yatu makes it easy to connect and find what you're looking for.

Our platform supports multiple user types including Tenants, Students, Businesses, Landlords, Property Owners, and Land Owners, each with tailored features and listings.
